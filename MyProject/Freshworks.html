FreshWorks



Freshworks, formerly Freshdesk, is a software company which develops and hosts help desk software and related customer support and knowledge base services Freshservice. Freshservice is Freshworks' IT service desk software. Freshservice is used primarily for IT and HR teams interested in getting a better handle on incident, SLA, task, inventory, and knowledge management through the use of automations, multi-channel support, and team collaboration features. Freshdesk is a software product based company. Now it is rebranded as Freshworks very recently. It has apps for small and medium businesses which cater those companies software need in customer support, Asset management, Sales & CRM and more. Freshdesk is a cloud-based customer support software and helpdesk solution. It is a platform with a comprehensive set of tools and features to help businesses support all the needs of their customers. Companies around the world use it to deliver exceptional customer service and experience.
