Sify



Sify Technologies Limited (formerly Satyam Infoway)[5] is an Indian information and communications technology company providing end to end ICT solutions including telecom services, data center services, cloud & managed services, transformation integration services and application integration services. Sify Technologies Limited played an important role during the early spread of Internet and e-commerce in India. It has been listed on NASDAQ as SIFY since October ,1999. Merill Lynch was the underwriter for Sify's IPO on the NASDAQ.[6] This company completed 23 years.
